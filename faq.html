Start with Confidence

Do I need to be good with computers?
No. Our classes are built for beginners. If you can click a mouse or tap a screen, you’re ready.
Can I use a phone or tablet instead of a computer?
Yes. Many tools we use work well on mobile devices. We’ll show you how to use whatever device you have.
How long are the classes?
Most classes are between 1 and 2 hours per week. Some are self-paced, so you can go at your own speed.
Is this free?
Some classes are free. Others may include a small fee. We’ll always let you know before you register.
What if I get stuck or feel overwhelmed?
That’s what Chip is for! Our AI helper will guide you step by step. You can also reach out to us anytime.

Still have questions?

Send us a message or give us a call—we’d love to help!
