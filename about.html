Why We Exist

Reboot 50+ was created to empower older adults with the knowledge, tools, and confidence to thrive in today’s digital world. We believe learning never stops, and age is never a limit to curiosity, creativity, or connection.

Who We Serve

We work with individuals aged 50 and older who want to learn about technology in a simple, encouraging, and non-intimidating environment.

Meet Chip

Chip is your AI-powered helper, built to guide you through this journey step-by-step with humor, patience, and plain-English explanations. You’ll see Chip throughout your learning experience, always ready to cheer you on.
